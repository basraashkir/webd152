Our Heritage

Pacific Trails Resort was born out of a love for nature and the desire to provide an unforgettable escape. Tucked into California's stunning North Coast, our resort blends modern comfort with rustic charm, offering the perfect base for those who seek quiet moments, outdoor beauty, and authentic trail experiences.

Adventure Awaits

Whether you're kayaking in the bay, hiking winding trails through old-growth forests, or relaxing in one of our handcrafted yurts, adventure lives in every step at Pacific Trails. Each path leads to something new — from shaded canyons to panoramic ocean overlooks.

For those who crave connection with the land, our guided nature walks and trail-side meditation spots invite you to slow down, breathe deep, and be present.

Your Journey Starts Here

Nestled between redwood groves and coastal cliffs, Pacific Trails Resort offers more than just a destination — it offers a path back to nature. Located just off scenic Highway 1, we're easy to reach and hard to forget.

Join Us for Your Perfect Getaway

Ready to explore winding coastal paths and peaceful forest trails? Whether you're traveling solo or with loved ones, Pacific Trails invites you to reconnect, recharge, and rediscover the beauty of the outdoors.
